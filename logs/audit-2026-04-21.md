## Findings

### 1. `sitemap.xml` severely outdated  [SEO]

**12 URLs listed, 27 articles exist.** Only 2 article URLs are in the sitemap (`silver-lining-protocol`, `medicinal-plants-your-galley`). Missing from sitemap:

```
banana-bread, claude-code-galley, cooked-fire-made-us-human,

how-to-become-a-yacht-chef, mushrooms-the-new-centre, myojaku-three-stars,
port-call-venice, preview-port-call, preview-signal-fire, preview-the-locker,
provisioning-miami-ftl, sea-urchin-beurre-blanc, sea-urchin-seasonal-guide,
spaghetti-ricci-di-mare, tea-as-cooking-ingredient, tech-in-the-galley,
the-acid-index, the-fat-index, the-salt-index, umami-western-cuisine,
whatsapp-trip-intelligence, yacht-crew-tax-guide
```

Google and Bing have no signal for 25 articles. `lastmod` stuck at 2026-03-01 across all listed URLs.

**Fix:** regenerate `sitemap.xml` from `data/articles.json`. Trivial to script.

### 2. `feed.xml` (RSS) outdated  [distribution]

**14 items in feed, 27 articles exist.** 13 missing from RSS. Subscribers see less than half of published content. `lastBuildDate` is 2026-04-07.

Missing from feed (sample): banana-bread, claude-code-galley, galley-efficiency-protocol, garlic-the-immortal-bulb, mushrooms-the-new-centre, port-call-venice, preview-*, provisioning-miami-ftl, tea-as-cooking-ingredient, whatsapp-trip-intelligence.

**Fix:** regenerate `feed.xml` from `data/articles.json`.

### 3. Unused Google Fonts preconnect  [minor perf, harmless]

`index.html` lines 21-22 preconnect to `fonts.googleapis.com` and `fonts.gstatic.com`, but `style.css` imports no Google Fonts (no `@import`, no `@font-face`, no `<link>` to Google). The preconnects fire one wasted DNS+TLS handshake per visit. Other pages in the site do use Google Fonts — this preconnect may have been copy-pasted; safe to delete on `index.html`.

### 4. Masthead logo a11y: duplicate announcement  [minor a11y]

`index.html:31` — the anchor contains an `<img>` with `alt="Littoralicious"` followed immediately by visible text `LITTORALICIOUS`. Screen readers announce "Littoralicious Littoralicious". Set `alt=""` on the logo image (decorative, text label is adjacent).
