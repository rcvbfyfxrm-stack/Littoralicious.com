Northern Adriatic

ITALY

Trieste

Gulf of Trieste · Friuli Venezia Giulia, Italy

A Habsburg free port where the katabatic bora files the air clean at 180 km/h and the table carries every empire that passed through — the boiled-pork buffet, the Viennese caffè vocabulary, and the Carso plateau’s native Vitovska and Terrano wines that the natural-wine world found in the 1990s.

ITALY
