## KEY INSIGHT

The chefs for whom the ocean is truly inseparable from identity share one trait: **they changed how we think about seafood, not just how we cook it.**

- Angel Leon proved you could eat plankton and farm the sea
- Josh Niland proved you could age fish like beef
- Olivier Roellinger proved chefs could save species
- Jiro Ono proved sushi is a lifetime practice
- Aitor Arregui proved simplicity is perfection

These are not seafood chefs. They are philosophers who happen to work with fish.
