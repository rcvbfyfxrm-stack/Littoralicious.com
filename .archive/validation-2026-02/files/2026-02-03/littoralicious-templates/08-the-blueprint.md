## TEMPLATE NOTES

### Voice & Style
- Active verbs: "Whisk" not "The ingredients should be whisked"
- Specific numbers: "175°C (350°F)" not "moderate oven"
- Visual cues: "golden brown" + "internal temp 95°C" — both
- No filler: cut "delicious," "amazing," "perfect"

### Required Elements
- [ ] Weight AND volume measurements (weight preferred)
- [ ] Temperatures in Celsius (Fahrenheit in parentheses)
- [ ] Times in minutes or hours, never "a while"
- [ ] At least one checkpoint in method
- [ ] Doneness indicator (temp, visual, tactile)
- [ ] At least 2 science explanations with sources
- [ ] At least 3 elevation options across tiers
- [ ] Prep-ahead timeline
- [ ] At least 2 service formats
- [ ] Troubleshooting section
- [ ] Quick reference card

### Sources to Prioritize
1. ChefSteps — precision techniques, sous vide
2. Serious Eats / Food Lab — tested recipes, science explanations
3. Modernist Cuisine — technique deep-dives
4. America's Test Kitchen — exhaustive testing
5. Harold McGee — foundational science
6. Ali Bouzari (Ingredient) — component science
7. Ratio (Ruhlman) — foundational ratios
8. The Flavor Bible — pairing ideas for elevation
