What is Littoralicious?

A digital publication for professional yacht chefs and serious cooks who want to understand why techniques work, not just how.

We combine peer-reviewed science with coastal culinary traditions from around the world.

One purpose: Nurture.

What you'll get each month

Shore Larder — Seasonal ingredients and sourcing intelligence
The Method — Science-backed techniques for the galley
Heritage — Coastal recipes from around the world
The Evidence — Research digests, papers translated for chefs
The Bridge — Industry news that matters

Who's behind this?

I'm Arnaud Callier. A decade in superyacht galleys. 50,000+ nautical miles. From M/Y Symphony to Vava II, Christina O., Cloud 9, and Kismet.

Years of feeding others taught me something: the best meals I ever made weren't the most impressive. They were the ones that left people feeling genuinely better.
