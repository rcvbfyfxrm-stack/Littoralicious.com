# Welcome to Littoralicious

You just joined something small, independent, and built for people who actually cook.

---

**What this is:** Think of your grandmother cooking — the one who could fix anything with a pan and a pinch of something — except she has a science degree and happened to spend her career on superyachts. That's the voice. Warm, precise, no nonsense. Free. No ads, no sponsors, no paywalls.

**What you'll get:** One email per month. The Monthly Brief. It connects what's in season, how to use it, why it works, where it came from, and what's happening in the industry. No filler.
