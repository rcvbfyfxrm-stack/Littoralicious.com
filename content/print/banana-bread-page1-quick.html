Banana Bread

Yield: 1 loaf (10-12 slices)
Active: 15 min | Total: 1 hr 15 min
Oven: 175°C (350°F)

Ingredients

Dry

All-purpose flour240g
Baking soda6g
Salt4g
Cinnamon (opt)2g

Wet

Very ripe bananas400g
Butter, melted115g
Brown sugar150g
White sugar50g
Eggs2
Vanilla10g
Greek yogurt60g

Optional

Walnuts, toasted100g
Chocolate chips100g

Method

  1. Prep: Oven 175°C. Line 9×5" pan with parchment.
  2. Dry: Whisk flour, soda, salt, cinnamon.
  3. Wet: Mash bananas. Add melted butter + sugars. Whisk in eggs, vanilla, yogurt.
  4. Combine: Fold dry into wet until just combined. Streaks OK.
  5. Bake: 175°C (350°F), 55-65 min.
  6. Done: 95°C internal. Moist crumbs on toothpick.
  7. Cool: 15 min in pan. 30 min on rack before slicing.

Elevation

Brown the butter+8 min
Add 30g white miso+0 min
Tahini swirl on top+2 min
Roast bananas first+25 min

Mise en Place

DRY KIT (keeps 3 months)
240g flour + 4g salt + 2g cinnamon + 150g brown sugar + 50g white sugar
No soda — add 6g fresh, fold last
FROZEN BATTER (keeps 6-8 weeks)
Complete batter → freeze in lined pan → vacuum seal block
Thaw overnight → 30 min room temp → bake 175°C

Storage

Room temp3 days
Fridge1 week
Freezer (baked)3 months

Troubleshooting

DenseOvermixed
Sunk middleUnderbaked
GummyToo wet bananas
