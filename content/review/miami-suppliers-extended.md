## Key Intel

- **Costco Business Center ≠ regular Costco** — professional packs, restaurant-format packaging. Worth the extra drive to Doral.
- **Halpern's is where provisioners source from** — go direct, skip the 15-18% agent markup.
- **168 Market = last reliable large Asian source** before the Caribbean. Stock up.
- **Stone crab season:** October-May at Captain Jim's and Haulover Marina.
- **Haulover Marina hack:** Boats come in noon and 17:00. Buy direct off the boat. Cash. 40-60% below retail.
- **Whole Foods free provisioning service:** Call ahead, they shop + store + hold. No markup.
- **Restaurant Depot membership:** Free. Boat registration + business card + captain's ID. Covers every location in the US.
- **Freezer strategy:** Wild Fork and Snake River Farms arrive already frozen + vac-packed = best quality for freezer loading.
- **Flour warning:** Stock 12%+ protein flour (Caputo, bread flour) — scarce and expensive in the Caribbean.
- **November-April:** Peak growing season in South Florida. Tomatoes, avocados, tropical fruits at their best and cheapest.
- **"All of these are much better than Bush Brothers"** — for premium meats, the specialist suppliers above outperform standard yacht provisioners significantly.
