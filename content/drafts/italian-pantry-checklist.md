## Budget Estimate

*Base: 8 PAX + 4 crew, 7-day charter*

| Tier | Description | Estimated Cost |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| **Essential** | Foundation pantry only | €350–450 |
| **Standard** | Essentials + key upgrades (finishing oil, premium pasta, some DOP tomatoes) | €500–650 |
| **Premium** | Full range including luxurious items (tradizionale balsamic, Cavalieri pasta, aged Parmigiano) | €750–950 |

*Does not include fresh items (mozzarella, herbs, produce) or proteins.*

## Order Timeline

| When | Action |
|------|--------|
| **2 weeks out** | Order Tradizionale balsamic, specialty oils, Cavalieri pasta (if not in stock locally) |
| **1 week out** | Order shelf-stable items: pasta, tomatoes, vinegars, canned goods |
| **48 hours out** | Order fresh mozzarella, burrata, herbs, ricotta |
| **Day of departure** | Receive fresh delivery, inspect quality, proper refrigerated storage |
| **Mid-charter (if needed)** | Restock fresh basil, mozzarella at port call |
