Sticky Toffee Pudding

Soda-bloomed dates. Dark muscovado & black treacle. Whisky caramel finished off-heat.

Yield: 9 portions (8″ square) Active: 15 min Total: 1 hr 15 min Oven: 170°C (340°F) Done: 94°C internal

Method

  1. Bloom dates. Pit and halve dates. Stir 6g soda into 280g boiling water, pour over dates. It foams. Stand 15 min. Blend smooth. Cool to ≤40°C. Soda raises pH from 5 to 8 → cell walls + fiber dissolve, fruit goes silky. Pre-reacts acid before flour. — McGee 2004
  2. Preheat 170°C (340°F). Butter + line 8″ pan with parchment sling. Whisk dry: flour, baking powder, salt.
  3. Cream butter + muscovado + treacle. 3–4 min until pale and aerated. Slight graininess fine. Muscovado = 15% molasses (vs 6% in light brown). Invert sugars keep crumb moist; molasses re-acidifies for residual lift. — Parks 2017
  4. Eggs one at a time + vanilla. Scrape between each.
  5. Fold in flour and dates in alternation: half flour → all date purée → remaining flour. 8–10 strokes max. Stop at 80% incorporated — flour streaks fine. Hot purée → melted butter → gummy crumb. Cool to 40°C. Overmixing develops gluten = tough.
  6. Bake 170°C, 30–35 min. Tap pan once after pouring to release air pockets.
Done: Internal 94°C (200°F). Skewer tacky with crumbs, not wet. Top matte and slightly cracked.
Rest: 10 min in pan. Do not unmould. Skewer all over (30+ holes) before saucing.
Sauce — make while pudding bakes
  1. a. Butter + muscovado, low heat, stir until dissolved — 3–4 min.
  2. b. Off heat: cream + vanilla, whisk smooth. Return low heat, simmer 90 sec.
  3. c. Off heat: whisky + flaky salt. Hold 70–80°C. Whisky on heat → aromatics boil off >78°C. — Modernist Cuisine
  4. d. Service: Pour 1/3 over warm pudding (60 sec absorb) → slice → drench 40g hot sauce per portion → cold cream.

Elevation

Tier 1 — No Extra Time
  • Silan glaze — replace treacle with date molasses
  • PX sherry instead of whisky — raisin/fig notes mirror date
  • Espresso compensator — 1g instant if skipping booze
Tier 2 — Worth It (+10 min)
  • Brown butter cake — brown 130g, weigh 100g
  • Burnt honey sauce — caramelize 30g honey, whisk in
  • Miso caramel — 15g shiro miso into finished sauce
Tier 3 — Restaurant
  • Smoked dates — cold-smoke 20 min over oak
  • Smoked clotted cream — 15 min hay/applewood
  • Bone marrow crust — 20g rendered marrow + flaky salt brushed on top

Troubleshoot

Metallic tasteSoda not dissolved. Stir to clear before pour.
Dense, gummyDate purée too hot. Cool to 40°C.
Sunk middleUnderbaked. Hit 94°C internal.
Sauce splitOff heat, splash cold cream, whisk hard.
Sauce too thick cooledWhisk in 1 tbsp warm cream / 100g sauce.
Cake tastes flatLight brown sugar → muscovado, or +25g molasses.
