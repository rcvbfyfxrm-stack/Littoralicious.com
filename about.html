Meet the Founder

Arnaud Callier — A decade in superyacht galleys. 50,000+ nautical miles. From M/Y Symphony to Vava II, Christina O., Cloud 9, and Kismet.

Years of feeding others taught me something: the best meals I ever made weren't the most impressive. They were the ones that left people feeling genuinely better. Calmer. More energized. More themselves.

That's what I want Littoralicious to be about. Not showing off. Not content for content's sake. Just the knowledge and tools to nurture people—body and mind—through the food we make.
