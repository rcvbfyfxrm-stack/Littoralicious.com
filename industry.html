Industry News
2026-01-26 · 10:00 UTC

Monaco Yacht Show 2026 Announces Dedicated Culinary Pavilion

For the first time, MYS will feature a standalone culinary pavilion showcasing galley equipment, provisioning partners, and live chef demonstrations. Applications for chef presentations open February 15.

2026-01-24 · 14:00 UTC

MYBA Chef Competition Returns to Antibes

The Mediterranean Yacht Brokers Association has confirmed the 2026 Superyacht Chef Competition will return to Port Vauban, Antibes on June 12-14. Registration opens March 1. Last year's winner, Chef Maria Santos from M/Y Serenity, will judge alongside three Michelin-starred chefs.

2026-01-22 · 09:00 UTC

PYA Updates Food Safety Certification Requirements

The Professional Yachting Association now requires HACCP Level 3 certification for head chefs on vessels over 50m. Existing chefs have until December 2026 to comply. Several online providers have been approved for remote certification.

2026-01-20 · 16:00 UTC

Crew4Yachts Reports 23% Increase in Chef Placement Requests

Recruitment platform data shows significant demand growth for qualified yacht chefs, with Mediterranean positions leading. Average day rates have increased 15% year-over-year. Dual-trained chef/stewardess roles seeing particular growth on vessels under 40m.
