Recipe

Chicken-Club Sandwich

Serves 4

Build

  • 500 g poached chicken breast, shredded
  • 3 tbsp Greek-yogurt caesar (yogurt, anchovy, lemon, parmesan, garlic)
  • 8 rashers crisped bacon · 2 baby gem, shredded
  • 30 g sun-dried tomatoes · 3 tbsp basil pesto
  • 8 slices sourdough or artisan loaf, toasted

Method

  1. Fold shredded chicken through the yogurt-caesar; season.
  2. Smear pesto on the base slice, pile chicken, bacon, gem, dried tomato.
  3. Close, press, cut on the diagonal. Serve with a lemon-dressed side salad.

Recipe

Summer Rice-Paper Rolls + Peanut-Hoisin Dip

Serves 4 · 12 rolls

Rolls

  • 12 rice-paper rounds
  • 300 g cooked prawns (or poached chicken / pressed tofu)
  • 100 g rice vermicelli, cooked & cooled
  • 1 mango + 1 avocado + 1 cucumber, julienned
  • Mint, coriander, Thai basil · butter lettuce

Dip

  • 3 tbsp peanut butter · 2 tbsp hoisin · 1 tbsp lime · splash water · chili to taste

Method

  1. Dip each round in warm water 5 sec until pliable; lay on a damp cloth.
  2. Lower third: herbs, lettuce, vermicelli, protein, mango, avo, cucumber.
  3. Fold sides in, roll tight. Whisk the dip smooth. Serve cold.

Recipe

Overnight Chia-Açaí Bowl

Serves 4

Night before

  • 6 tbsp chia seeds · 500 ml milk of choice · 1 tsp vanilla · 1 tbsp maple

To serve

  • 200 g frozen açaí (or blended frozen berries + banana)
  • Greek yogurt · granola · fresh berries · banana · peanut butter drizzle

Method

  1. Whisk chia, milk, vanilla, maple; chill overnight until set.
  2. Blend açaí with a splash of milk to a thick sorbet.
  3. Layer chia, açaí, yogurt; top with granola, fruit, peanut drizzle.

Recipe

Chipotle Chicken + Pesto Green Salad

Serves 4

Chicken

  • 4 chicken thighs · 1 tbsp chipotle paste · 1 tbsp olive oil · lime · garlic · oregano

Salad

  • Mixed leaves, baby gem · cucumber · radish · pumpkin seeds
  • Pesto dressing: 2 tbsp pesto + 1 tbsp olive oil + 1 tbsp lemon + 1 tbsp water

Method

  1. Marinate chicken 30 min in chipotle, oil, lime, garlic, oregano.
  2. Sear hot then finish in a 200°C oven ~12 min; rest, slice.
  3. Loosen the pesto, toss the leaves, plate the chicken alongside. (Sourdough optional — skip for low-carb.)

Recipe

Peanut-Chili Lentils + Sesame Greens

Serves 4

Bowl

  • 250 g cooked puy lentils · 200 g cooked quinoa
  • 2 handfuls kale, massaged with lemon & salt
  • Tenderstem broccoli, blanched · pickled red onion · coriander

Peanut-chili sauce

  • 2 tbsp peanut butter · 1 tbsp soy · 1 tsp chili crisp · 1 tbsp lime · 1 tsp honey · water
  • Sesame greens dressing: 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil + 1 tsp rice vinegar + sesame seeds

Method

  1. Whisk the peanut-chili sauce; fold half through warm lentils.
  2. Toss greens in the sesame dressing.
  3. Build the bowl: quinoa, kale, lentils, greens; spoon over remaining sauce, scatter seeds.

Recipe

Roast Chicken, Sweet Potato & Tahini-Lemon

Serves 4

Bowl

  • 4 chicken thighs, roasted with smoked paprika
  • 2 sweet potatoes, cubed & roasted · 200 g brown rice, cooked
  • Tenderstem & kale, charred in a dry pan

Tahini-lemon

  • 3 tbsp tahini · 2 tbsp lemon · 1 small garlic, grated · water to drizzle · salt

Method

  1. Roast chicken & sweet potato at 200°C until caramelised (~25 min).
  2. Char the greens hard in a hot dry pan.
  3. Bowl up over brown rice; flood with tahini-lemon.

Recipe

Salmon Poke Bowl

Serves 4

Bowl

  • 500 g sashimi-grade salmon, cubed
  • Marinade: 3 tbsp soy · 1 tbsp sesame oil · 1 tsp rice vinegar · 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 300 g sushi or brown rice · edamame · cucumber · avocado · radish · nori
  • Top: pickled ginger · spring onion · sesame · sriracha-yogurt

Method

  1. Toss salmon in the marinade 10 min (no longer — it firms).
  2. Cool the rice to room temp; arrange toppings in lanes.
  3. Crown with salmon; drizzle sriracha-yogurt, scatter sesame & nori.

Recipe

Smashed Avocado & Poached Eggs on Sourdough

Serves 4

Build

  • 3 ripe avocados · juice of 1 lime · chili flakes · salt
  • 8 eggs · splash of vinegar (poaching water)
  • 8 slices sourdough, toasted · feta · radish · coriander · dukkah

Method

  1. Smash avocado with lime, chili and salt — keep it chunky.
  2. Poach eggs in barely-simmering vinegared water ~3 min for runny yolks.
  3. Pile avo on toast, crown with eggs, crumble feta, scatter radish, coriander & dukkah.

Recipe

Grilled Chicken & Brown-Rice Box

Serves 4

Box

  • 4 chicken breasts, butterflied · 1 tbsp olive oil · garlic · lemon · thyme
  • 300 g brown rice, cooked with a bay leaf
  • Slaw: white cabbage, carrot, lemon-yogurt · cherry tomatoes · cucumber

Garlic-lemon drizzle

  • 3 tbsp Greek yogurt · 1 garlic, grated · 1 tbsp lemon · parsley

Method

  1. Marinate chicken in oil, garlic, lemon, thyme; griddle ~4 min/side, rest, slice.
  2. Fork the brown rice; dress the slaw.
  3. Box up rice, chicken, slaw, tomato & cucumber; spoon over garlic-lemon.
