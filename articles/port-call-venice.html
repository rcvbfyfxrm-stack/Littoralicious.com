Port Call Venice: Provisioning a City With No Roads

No cars. No delivery vans. Everything by boat or on foot. Context: How Venice Actually Works

"No road vehicles on the island. Everything moves by boat or on foot.
A van full of provisions in Mestre cannot drive to the boat."

The Vaporetto in Three Lines

  • Line 2 — Grand Canal express. San Marco ↔ Rialto in ~15 min.
  • Lines 4.1 / 5.1 — Circular. Sant'Elena ↔ Piazzale Roma. The mainland connector.
  • Line 13 — Fondamente Nove ↔ Sant'Erasmo. ~45 min. The direct-supply line.

2026 fares: single €9.50 / 75 min; 24h €25; 48h €35; 72h €45; 7-day €65. ACTV info: +39 041 041.

The Carrello

Free baggage allowance: three pieces (two within 120 cm L+W+H, one within 150 cm). A standard granny carrello fits inside that envelope. Don't try it during commuter rush — before 8 AM or after 7 PM is the realistic window.

Water Taxis

For serious provisioning a motoscafo picks you up, takes you to the market, waits, and brings you back loaded. Indicative costs:

  • Short inner-city trip: €60–90
  • Provisioning run (Rialto to yacht, loaded): €150–250 negotiated flat rate
  • Piazzale Roma with heavy boxes: €120–180

Negotiate before boarding. Day dispatch +39 041 240 6712, night/weekend +39 041 522 2303.

Venice Access Fee

Day-tripper fee: €5 (booked ≥4 days ahead) or €10 (within 3 days), selected Fri–Sun + bridge weekends, 08:30–16:00. 2026 dates: 3 Apr–26 Jul. Yacht crew on a working provisioning run almost certainly fall under the "workers operating in the historic city" exemption — get it in writing through your agent or via cda.veneziaunica.it.

What to Buy in Venice That You Can't Get Elsewhere

Castraure (Sant'Erasmo)
~10-day window, last week of April into early May. Apical buds — raw with olive oil and Parmigiano. Order direct from I Sapori di Sant'Erasmo.
Moeche (soft-shell crab)
Spring + autumn windows. 2025 was a failed season — ask the moecanti what they actually landed before you commit a course.
White Asparagus Bassano DOP
19 March–13 June 2026, peak April. Hand-harvested, single-farm traceable.
Prosecco DOCG Conegliano-Valdobbiadene
UNESCO hills, an hour north. Ask by name for Cartizze or Rive. Buy from Mascari or Schiavi.

Out-of-season warnings (April–May call)

Radicchio di Treviso Tardivo IGP — real season Nov–March. Zucchini flowers — a summer ingredient.
