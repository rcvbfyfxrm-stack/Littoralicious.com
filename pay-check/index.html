            How this is worked out. Base bands interpolate the 2026 superyacht crew salary guides (Foreland Marine, Yotspot, YPI CREW, Lighthouse, Yachtly) by vessel length and role, then adjust for programme, rotation, experience and (for a sole chef) any second role. Tips follow the MYBA convention (15–20% of the charter fee, split across crew). Scope and rest-hour notes follow the Maritime Labour Convention 2006 and anonymised crew reporting. Figures are a guide for negotiation, not financial advice — the package and itinerary move the real number.
